# 👥 User Personas: המתכונים של מרינה (Marina's Recipes)

> **Purpose:** These personas guide "The Golden Loop" and ensure the product resonates with its intended audience, providing a warm, family-friendly Hebrew experience.

---

## 1. Persona: [Pending User Input - e.g., The Busy Parent / The Home Cook]
*The primary user who will spend the most time in the application.*

- **Bio:** [Pending User Input - e.g., Someone looking to put a good meal on the table without spending hours searching for the right recipe.]
- **Technical Literacy:** [Pending User Input - e.g., Average / Needs a highly intuitive interface.]
- **Core Motivation:** To quickly find a recipe that fits the ingredients they have or the dietary needs of their family (e.g., Kid Friendly, Not Spicy).

### Goals & Pain Points
- **Goals:**
  - Find a recipe in under a minute using precise filters.
  - Read instructions clearly without being overwhelmed by clutter.
- **Pain Points:**
  - Current recipe sites are full of ads and long life-stories before the actual ingredients.
  - Hard to filter specifically for combinations like "Vegan" + "Quick" + "Kid Friendly" in Hebrew.

### "Vibe" Resonance
*What aesthetic or functional 'feeling' makes this user feel at home?*
- Needs a warm, inviting interface (Terracotta/Orange tones).
- Soft edges (8px) and a clean, spacious layout that feels stress-free.

---

## 2. Persona: The Dietary-Specific Searcher
*A secondary user with strict dietary preferences.*

- **Bio:** An individual who specifically searches for "Healthy" or "Vegan" recipes and needs to know exactly what goes into their food.
- **Technical Literacy:** Average to High.
- **Core Motivation:** To discover new, exciting meals that fit their lifestyle restrictions.

### Goals & Pain Points
- **Goals:**
  - Trust the labeling system (Vegan, Dairy, Meat).
  - Easily exclude unwanted ingredients.
- **Pain Points:**
  - Accidentally finding a recipe that contains a restricted ingredient hidden in the text.

### "Vibe" Resonance
- Clear, highly visible pill-shaped tags for labels (Vegan, Healthy).

---

## 3. User Stories
*Translating personas into actionable development tasks.*

- **As a user,** I want to filter by "Kids Friendly" and "Not Spicy", so that I can quickly find a dinner my children will actually eat.
- **As a user,** I want to filter by "Main Material: Fish" and "Complexity: Beginner", so that I can learn to cook a new protein without failing.
- **As a user,** I want the interface to be fully in Hebrew, so that I can easily read the ingredients and steps while cooking.
